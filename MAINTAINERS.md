## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues and bug reports
- Ensuring implementation conformance with the specification
- Managing releases and versioning
- Maintaining CI/CD and project infrastructure

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainer status is granted by the protocol author based on sustained, high-quality contributions to the project. There is no formal application process — contributors who demonstrate deep understanding of the protocol and consistent engagement will be considered.
